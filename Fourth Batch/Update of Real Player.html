  2. Click the ‘File’ button at the top of the window. If you do not see a ‘File’ button, click the ‘Real Player’ button on the upper-left part of the window.
  3. If you are on a Windows computer, select 'New' and then 'New Browser Window'. If you are on a Mac, select 'New Browser'.
  4. In the new Real Player browser that opens, go to www.kaptest.com and try to view your On Demand videos through this browser window.
